Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: September 23, 2025

PhotoInX (“App”) is provided by Tarık Nasuhoğlu (“Developer”). This policy explains what we process, how, and why.

Data We Collect

  • Diagnostics / Analytics: Crash reports and basic usage metrics (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics). These are not linked to your identity and are used to improve reliability and performance.
  • Media (On-device): Photos you select are processed locally on your device; we do not upload them to any server or cloud.

How We Use Data

  • Bug fixing, performance and reliability improvements
  • Customer support (if you contact us)

Sharing / Third Parties

Diagnostics may be processed by providers (e.g., Google/Firebase) under their own privacy policies and agreements.

Permissions

  • Camera / Photos: Local processing only.
  • Notifications (if any): For reminders, etc.

Retention

Diagnostics are retained for a reasonable period; media remains on your device and is not stored by us.
